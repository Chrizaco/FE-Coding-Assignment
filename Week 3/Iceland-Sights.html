Iceland Sights

Iceland is known for its beutiful scenes of nature. Due to its geographical location it has a phenomenon known as midnight sun which has the sky featuring many colors such as red and purple. Iceland's volcanoes define the nature of the land, creating endless fields of moss-coated lava, sweeping plains of black sand, jagged peaks, hot springs, geysers, and vast craters.
